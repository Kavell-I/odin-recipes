Instant Ramen

Is an explanation even needed?

Things you will need

  • Instant ramen($2 depending where you live)
  • Tap water(can be substituted for bottled water if you want to be fancy)
  • A kettle or a stove

Preparation

  1. Open the instant ramen and season it with the packet
  2. Boil some water and fill it 3/4 of the way up and wait 3-5 minute
  3. Voila you have your instant ramen now
